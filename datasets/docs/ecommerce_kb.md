# E-Commerce Knowledge Base

## Returns and Refunds

Customers can return any product within 30 days of the original purchase date for a full refund. Items must be returned in their original condition and packaging, unused and undamaged. Products that have been opened, used, or damaged by the customer are not eligible for a full refund but may qualify for a partial refund at our discretion.

Digital products, downloadable software, and gift cards are non-refundable under any circumstances. Perishable goods, personalised items, and hazardous materials are also excluded from our return policy.

To initiate a return, customers must contact our support team at support@example.com or via the Help Centre in their account dashboard. They will receive a return authorisation number and instructions within 24 hours. Returns sent without an authorisation number will be refused and returned to the sender.

Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The refund is issued to the original payment method. Credit card refunds may take an additional 3 to 5 business days to appear on statements depending on the issuing bank.

Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer unless the item was defective, damaged in transit, or incorrectly shipped. In those cases we will provide a prepaid return label and ship a replacement at no extra cost.

## Shipping and Delivery

We offer three standard shipping options for domestic orders: standard delivery (5 to 7 business days), express delivery (2 to 3 business days), and next-day delivery (order before 1 PM on a business day). International shipping is available to over 50 countries and typically takes 7 to 21 business days depending on destination and customs processing.

Orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping within the domestic region. This threshold does not apply to express or next-day delivery options. Free shipping promotions cannot be combined with other discount codes unless explicitly stated.

Once an order is dispatched, customers receive a tracking number via email. They can track their shipment directly through our website or via the carrier's tracking portal. Tracking information may take up to 24 hours to activate after the shipping label is created.

We are not responsible for delays caused by customs inspections, natural disasters, carrier strikes, or other circumstances outside our control. If a package is marked as delivered but not received, customers must report this within 48 hours so we can open an investigation with the carrier.

## Pricing and Payments

All prices displayed on the website are in US dollars and include applicable taxes unless otherwise stated at checkout. Prices are subject to change without notice. The price charged is the price displayed at the time the order is placed.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers for orders over $500. Cryptocurrency payments are not currently supported. Payment is charged at the time of order confirmation, not at dispatch.

If a payment fails, the order is held for 24 hours. Customers will receive an automated email with a secure link to retry the payment. After 24 hours the order is automatically cancelled and any reserved stock is released.

Promotional discount codes must be entered at checkout and cannot be applied retroactively to completed orders. Only one discount code can be used per order. Codes are case-insensitive and single-use unless explicitly stated otherwise.

## Products and Inventory

Our product catalogue is updated daily. Stock levels shown on product pages are approximate and updated every 15 minutes. A product displayed as in stock may occasionally become unavailable between the time it is added to the cart and the time the order is confirmed. In such cases, customers are notified immediately and offered a full refund or the option to wait for restock.

Product descriptions, images, and specifications are provided by suppliers and are subject to change. We make every effort to display products accurately but cannot guarantee that colours and textures appear exactly as in person due to display differences across devices.

Pre-order products have an estimated ship date displayed on the product page. This date is an estimate and may change. Pre-order customers are notified by email of any date changes. Pre-orders can be cancelled for a full refund at any point before the item ships.

## Customer Accounts

Creating an account is optional but recommended. Account holders can view order history, track shipments, save multiple delivery addresses, manage payment methods, and receive personalised product recommendations. Guest checkout is available for all orders.

Passwords must be at least 8 characters and include at least one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character. We recommend using a unique password not used on other websites. Two-factor authentication is available and strongly recommended.

If a customer suspects unauthorised access to their account, they should change their password immediately and contact support. We will never ask for a full password via email, chat, or phone. Account data can be exported or deleted at any time in accordance with our privacy policy and applicable data protection regulations.

## Customer Support

Our support team is available Monday to Friday from 8 AM to 8 PM EST and Saturday from 9 AM to 5 PM EST. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays. Average response time is under 2 hours during business hours and under 12 hours outside business hours.

Support is available via live chat on the website, email at support@example.com, and phone at 1-800-555-0100. For complex issues involving account security or payments, we recommend calling directly rather than using chat. All communications are logged for quality assurance.

Customers with a Premium or Business account receive priority support with guaranteed responses within 30 minutes during business hours and access to a dedicated account manager for orders over $10,000.
